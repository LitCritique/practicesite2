Personal Touch

I’ve been teaching for 16 years, and I still get excited about classroom debates and a new book to share with my students. English is my favorite subject to teach because it gives students something priceless: a voice. Once they know how to express themselves, they can do anything! Accomplish anything! I love mixing the old with the new—pairing Shakespeare with slam poetry, or Austen with modern memoirs. My classroom is a space for open-floor talks, project-based learning, and the occasional accidental joke (my students say I’m “quirky and accidentally funny”—I’ll take it!).
